Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to TechVyro. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI SaaS platform.

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (encrypted) when you sign up.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our AI tools (e.g., number of credits used, types of tools accessed).
  • Content Data: Text prompts and inputs you provide to generate AI content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our AI services.
  • To manage your account and credit balance.

  • To communicate with you regarding updates or support.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your passwords are hashed, and our connections are secured via SSL encryption. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. AI Content Generation

Please note that text or images generated by our AI tools are processed by advanced machine learning models. While we strive for privacy, we recommend not inputting sensitive personal or financial information into the AI prompts.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session (keep you logged in) and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser.
